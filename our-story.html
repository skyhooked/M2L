The M2 Labs Story

M2 Labs is a combination of Nick’s family history and Jonathon’s last name. The “M2,” referred to as “Mike Squared,” honours the RAF Avro Lancaster B Mk. III Heavy Bomber – ED888/PM‑M2 “Mike Squared,” No. 103 Squadron, RAF Elsham Wolds, Lincoln, which Nick’s grandfather, serving with the 103rd Squadron, was the last to fly after WWII, with “M2” painted on the fuselage. The “Labs” portion of M2 Labs combines Jonathon’s last name, “Labadie,” and reflects the idea of a laboratory in their pursuit of innovative technology and products【112856937564002†L30-L45】.

Nick’s Story

Nick’s journey began in the broadcast industry on Australia’s East Coast, where he mastered the essentials of RF technology, electrical engineering and the art of keeping a network running smoothly. Music was always in the background – whether he was listening or jamming with friends. As his career progressed, Nick sharpened his skills in streamlining manufacturing, opening several factories in Hong Kong for leading brands in the RF Test & Measurement space. This experience eventually led him to the United States, where he applied his expertise and learned to thrive in the competitive RF industry【112856937564002†L49-L58】.

Nick’s innovative approach to designing and manufacturing equipment helped solve problems the industry didn’t even realise it had. After several career moves, Nick took the leap and started his own business. He developed a groundbreaking way to filter RF frequencies in a form factor a tenth the size of traditional equipment. It was during this time that he crossed paths with Jonathon through collaborating companies in the RF space, sparking a connection that would shape the next chapter of their careers【112856937564002†L59-L63】.

Jonathan’s Story

Jonathon’s passion for music began in childhood. Growing up in rural Georgia, he often dreamed of bigger possibilities. At thirteen he picked up the guitar and was instantly hooked. In his twenties he pursued his professional dreams with determination, gaining valuable experience across different fields – from graphic design to watchmaking and the service industry. These diverse paths shaped him into who he is today. All the while, Jonathon played countless shows with several bands, fuelling his deep love for performing【112856937564002†L67-L74】.

His passion eventually expanded into music production and recording, where he found new ways to express his creativity and hone his craft. In 2014, Jonathon crossed paths with Nick while working for a company that distributed Nick’s RF filters to the wireless market. Nick quickly became a mentor, offering guidance, knowledge and experience that further fuelled Jonathon’s growth both personally and professionally【112856937564002†L74-L79】.

The Beginning of M2 Labs

Jonathon and Nick knew from the start they wanted to work together. Right from that first conversation, it was clear they shared the same vision and respect for each other’s skills. Jonathon, full of fresh ideas and passion in the wireless market, found in Nick a mentor who not only understood the RF business but saw promise in Jonathon’s raw talent. Nick’s mentorship went beyond just passing on knowledge. It was about collaboration and encouraging Jonathon to think outside the box. They spent hours brainstorming, trying out new approaches and challenging each other’s perspectives. Nick was always hands‑on, working side by side with Jonathon on projects, showing him how theory translates into practice. Over time their partnership grew into a strong, dynamic synergy, blending Jonathon’s fresh outlook with Nick’s expertise【112856937564002†L91-L97】.

Ten years later, their journey led to the creation of M2 Labs. This wasn’t just another business – it was the result of their combined dreams and aspirations. They wanted M2 Labs to be a place where musicians could find the tools they needed to express themselves in new ways. Their goal was to bridge the gap between traditional music production and cutting‑edge technology, offering products that were innovative yet easy to use. They hoped M2 Labs would quickly become a go‑to for musicians wanting to push creative boundaries, a place where technology and creativity work together seamlessly【112856937564002†L100-L107】.

The spirit of M2 Labs reflects the journey Nick and Jonathon have been on. Their products showcase the craftsmanship and innovation they’ve honed over the years, with each one designed to meet – and even exceed – the needs of musicians. Their shared vision of empowering musicians through technology is evident in every aspect of the company.
